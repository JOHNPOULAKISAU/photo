Wedding Photographer. I Capture Life...

John Poulakis is a Sydney-based wedding photographer with over 7 years of experience in capturing the most important moments of your big day. He specializes in full coverage from preparation to reception, as well as engagement and post-wedding sessions.
Known for his creative and artistic style, John uses advanced lighting and composition techniques to create unique and memorable images. He also offers high-quality albums and other products to help you preserve your memories forever.
His prices are competitive and vary depending on the package chosen. Discounts are available for off-season weddings and early bookings. For more information about his services and pricing, visit his website or contact him directly.
Thank you for considering John Poulakis for your special event!
